My Awesome Cart!

My Awesome Cart Pvt Ltd. is an Indian electronic commerce company based in Vadodara, Gujarat India. Founded by Harsh Mayani in 2023, the company initially focused on book sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, and lifestyle products.The service competes primarily with Amazon' s Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal.My Awesome Cart held a 39.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry.My Awesome Cart is significantly dominant in the sale of apparel and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.My Awesome Cart also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI).In August 2023, U.S.-based retail chain Walmart acquired a 77% controlling stake in My Awesome Cart for US$16 billion, valuing it at $22 billion.
